Rules:

Ping Pong will count up to your number from 1 with the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 become "ping"
  2. Number divisilbe by 5 become "pong"
  3. Number divisible by 3 and 5 become "ping-pong"
(must be more than one digit long)
